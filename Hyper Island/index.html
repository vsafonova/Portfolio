Unrealistic beauty standarts

With the rise of users on Instagram, the platform has become a breeding ground for unrealistic and unreachable beauty standards, that negatively impact individuals' and society's as a whole mental health, self-esteem, and body image

If you analyze users' behavior (or simply open offers in social networks), then you can conclude that users with an appearance that often consists of the same elements receive wide recognition in social networks: a matte face without a single pimple, smoothed pores, emphasized cheekbones, smooth lip contour, and lush lips, "cat's" eyes with long, thick eyelashes, and lush, well-groomed eyebrows.
A taut hourglass figure with pronounced abs and voluminous breasts and buttocks. Long shiny hair, tanned skin, and naturally toned makeup.

In order to achieve wide recognition on Instagram, people use various face filters, use photoshop to make the waist look narrower and chest to fit this "beautiful" picture on Instagram.

How to solve these problems

Instagram could introduce several changes to their platform

Firstly, Instagram should take responsibility and implement measures to regulate content that promotes unrealistic beauty standards. They can do this by developing and enforcing policies that ban filters and photo-editing tools that promote unrealistic beauty standarts.

Secondly, they can highlight and promote diverse and inclusive beauty standards by showcasing individuals with different body types, skin tones, and features in the recommendation feed.

Thirdly, allow viewers to disable a face filter to see the original content.

These solutions would likely be introduced as digital products or features within the Instagram app

Instagram could work with mental health experts, activists, and influencers to create and promote these features

Some possible challenges with implementing these solutions include resistance from individuals

who do not believe that these are problems, technical limitations in creating features that effectively combat these problems, and potential loss of revenue if these changes are unpopular with advertisers.

The beneficiaries of these solutions would be Instagram users, especially those who are vulnerable to the negative effects of the platform

By promoting body positivity, Instagram can help create a more inclusive and positive environment for their users. This could lead to better mental health outcomes, increased engagement, and a more loyal user base.
